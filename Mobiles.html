Mobile Phones Analysis

Project Overview: This project focuses on analyzing mobile phone sales data to uncover trends, customer preferences, and key features that drive sales. The dataset includes information on various mobile phone models, their specifications, sales figures, and customer reviews. The goal is to provide insights that can help manufacturers and retailers understand market dynamics and customer needs.

Tools Used: Excel for data cleaning and preprocessing, PowerBI for creating a dynamic dashboard

Brands Analysis

The first part of analysis, it focuses on comparing different mobile phone brands based on specs, prices and ratings to understand which brands are leading the market and why. Key insights include:

  • Prices have positive correlation with screen size, with luxury mobile phones being an exceptions
  • Battery power correlates with price
  • Vertu is the most expensive mobile phone brand
  • Xiaomi is the highest rating phone brand

Phones Analysis

This part of the analysis dives deeper into individual phone models. It aims to find the best, highest rated, most expensive phone for each brand:

  • Xiaomi Redmi 14 is the most trending & highest rating phone with a rating of 4.8
  • Phones with no FM radio have a slightly lower overall rating
  • Small screen sized phones have a much lower overall rating, with luxury phones being exceptions
