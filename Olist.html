Business Insights & Executive Report for Olist

Project Overview: As a part of my Elevvo Pathways Data Analysis Intenship, I worked on an analytics project focused on analyzing business insights for Olist. Given a large dataset contaning:

  • A Table of Olist's customers information
  • Another table for products & their details
  • Orders table that shows details about orders, such as total value, product purchased and customer making the purchase
  • More tables that contains additional information about orders, such as reviews & payment methods

The goal of this project is to conduct a full-cycle analysis on this e-commerce dataset, by cleaning, preprocessing, analyzing the data and finally creating an executive report to showcase the key insights uncovered and provide recommendations based on the findings that drives business decisions.

Tools Used for Project: Python for data cleaning & preprocessing, PowerBI for creating an executive report

Revenue & Orders Overview

The first & main part of our analysis, it explores insights about Olist's orders and revenue to get a general overview of the business performance. Key insights:

  • Average Olist order value is 166 BRL (Brazilian Real)
  • Total revenue is 16M BRL from a total of 99.4k Orders
  • Average Order Freight value is 20 BRL, which is about 12% of the average order value
  • Revenue & Purchase Count Drops during the final quarter of the year, possibly due to holiday seasons. and peaks during summer
  • Sao Paulo is the state with the highest revenue and order count, followed by rio de janeiro and Minas Gerais
  • Revenue & Orders count keeps increasing during time, with august 2018 being the highest month for both metrics

Products Analysis

Aims to analyze Olist's products and their performance, key insights are:

  • Olist has a total of 32.95k products and 71 product categories, with health & beauty being the most sold category and cds/dvds being the highest rated category
  • Olist has an average delivery time of 12 days
  • 3.1k Sellers are registered on Olist platform
  • Sao paulo is the state with the highest number of sellers, including 1.2k sellers from the city of Sao Paulo itself
  • Highest selling product category is bed & bath, followed by health & beauty and sports leisure

Customers Analysis

The goal is to analyze Olist's customers and their behavior, key insights are:

  • Olist has a total of 96.46k customers, with 3.36% of them being repeat customers
  • Average shipping time is 9.28 days
  • Average review score for products is 4.1 out of 5
  • most orders occur on mondays & tuesdays, suggesting that customers prefer to shop at the beginning of the week
  • Most customers come from sao paulo, followed by minas gerais and rio de janeiro
  • Near half of Olist's customers had only 1 payment installment, with only 10.2% of customers opting for 8 or more instalments
